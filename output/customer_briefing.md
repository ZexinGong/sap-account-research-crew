# Customer Briefing: Nvidia *Prepared for SAP Account Executive — April 17, 2026*
## Company Snapshot 
Nvidia Corporation is a leading American technology company headquartered in Santa Clara, California, specializing in the development of graphics processing units (GPUs), central processing units (CPUs), and system-on-a-chip units. Founded in 1993, Nvidia is the world leader in accelerated computing and AI. With a strong growth trajectory, the company is poised for continued expansion, particularly in the areas of AI and quantum computing. Nvidia's financial health signals a robust growth trajectory, with data center revenues projected to maintain a remarkable compound annual growth rate (CAGR).

## What's Happening Right Now 
Nvidia has recently launched Ising, the world's first open AI models to accelerate the path to useful quantum computers. Additionally, the company has announced plans to power India's AI factories with tens of thousands of AI chips. Nvidia's CEO, Jensen Huang, has also urged companies to prioritize AI breakthroughs over profit, indicating a significant focus on innovation and ethical leadership in the technology sector.

## Strategic Priorities & SAP Relevance 
- *Complex Supply Chain Management*: SAP's Advanced Planner and Optimizer (APO) and SAP Integrated Business Planning (IBP) can help optimize Nvidia's global supply chain, addressing challenges such as managing a network of suppliers, distributors, and customers.
- *Innovative Product Development and Lifecycle Management*: SAP's Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) and SAP S/4HANA can streamline product development processes, from design to manufacturing, improving collaboration across departments.
- *Financial Planning and Analysis for High Growth*: SAP's Financial Planning and Analysis (FP&A) solutions, including SAP Analytics Cloud, can provide Nvidia with real-time insights and predictive analytics to support informed decision-making and manage its financial health effectively.
- *Talent Management for Skilled Workforce*: SAP SuccessFactors can aid in strategic human capital management, including recruitment, performance management, and learning development, ensuring Nvidia attracts and retains top talent in AI and quantum computing.
